2. Hosting and Infrastructure

Cloudflare

We use the services of Cloudflare, Inc. (101 Townsend St., San Francisco, CA 94107, USA) for our domain. Cloudflare acts as a Content Delivery Network (CDN) and security service. All requests to our website are routed through Cloudflare's infrastructure. In this process, IP addresses are processed to ensure the security and speed of our site. This is done based on our legitimate interest in the secure operation of our website (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR). We have entered into a Data Processing Addendum (DPA) with Cloudflare.

Cloudflare R2 (Media Streaming)

For hosting and streaming media files (e.g., videos or audio content), we use the storage service Cloudflare R2. When you access media content on our website, it is loaded directly from Cloudflare's servers (storage). For technical reasons, your IP address is transmitted to Cloudflare to enable the delivery of the content to your browser. This is done based on our legitimate interest in the high-performance and reliable provision of audio and video content (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR).

GitHub Pages

This website is hosted as a static site on the servers of GitHub Inc. (88 Colin P. Kelly Jr. St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). When you access this site, GitHub automatically collects information (log files), including your IP address, to enable the delivery of the website. This is based on our legitimate interest in a secure and efficient operation of our website (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR).

Design Resources (Tailwind CSS & Lucide Icons)

For the consistent presentation of the site, we use design resources (Tailwind CSS) and symbols (Lucide Icons). These are installed locally on our server. When loading these resources, no connection is established to external third-party servers, and therefore no transfer of your IP address to external CDN providers takes place.
