Offline Functionality (Cache Storage & Service Workers)

To enable offline use (e.g., airplane mode), we utilize modern browser technologies such as the Cache Storage API and Service Workers.

  • Purpose: Selected audio files (blobs) and the application logic are transferred directly into your browser's local memory. This enables zero-latency playback and ensures that the app remains functional without an internet connection.
  • Control: No personal data is transferred to this cache. You maintain full sovereignty: You can clear this local storage at any time via the "Purge" button in the app or your browser settings.
  • Legal Basis: This storage is based on our legitimate interest in providing our content in a technically self-sufficient manner (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR).
